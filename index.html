Welcome to the MERV Hotel

We're in the heart of the City across from the marina on 8 acres of beach property. We can arrange sailing trips, kayaking, snorkeling, and more adventures. Our beach is adjacent to the hotel, with private cabanas and a tiki bar on the sand.

For your dining experience, we have two restaurants; The Garden Cafe, which faces the beach and offers breakfast, lunch, or casual dinner. The Bayside dining room has lunch and fine dining.

Our conference and banquet rooms can accommodate meetings and events, with catering available at your request.

We have many amenities and comforts at the Merv Hotel for your business and leisure stay. We look forward to your arrival.
