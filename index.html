Welcome to the MERV Hotel

The hotel has a beautiful view of the marina, where you can enjoy sailing, with the guidance of our captain or if experienced on your own. Many of our rooms have balconies and face the marina. Adjacent to the hotel, a wide beach with light golden sand stretches connecting many of the hotels on the strip. A walkway takes you from our hotel to the other hotels, and along the way are small shops where you can stop for ice cream or a snack. For your dining experience, we offer two restaurants; The Garden cafe faces the beach and offers breakfast, lunch, or casual dinner. The Bayside dining room offers fine dining. We have many amenities and; comforts for your business and leisure stay. Our conference rooms of different sizes can accommodate various types of meetings and catering is available at your request. The fully equipped gym is open 24 hours for your convenience. We look forward to your stay at the MERV Hotel.
